OpenCongress Has Been Retired

The Sunlight Foundation has retired OpenCongress. This link will redirect you to GovTrack.us, a similarly nonpartisan source of free, comprehensive legislative data and information. Read more about this transition here.

GovTrack now incorporates Email Congress, a service powered by the Sunlight Foundation that allows constituents to email their members of Congress. You can also access Email Congress at emailcongress.us.

We're proud of the ground we, and others who have contributed to OpenCongress, have broken. Because of projects like OpenCongress, legislative data and information are now more widely available than ever before. Thank you for your support!
